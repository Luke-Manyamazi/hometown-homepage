Welcome to Cape Town

Cape Town is a modern, cosmopolitan city surrounded by nature and Table Mountain, one of the New 7 Wonders of the world.

Top Six Things to Do in Cape Town

Cable Car

The Table Mountain Aerial Cableway offers visitors a five-minute ride to the top of Table Mountain in Cape Town.

The Bo-Kaap

The Bo-Kaap is an area formerly known as the Malay Quarter, situated on the slopes of Signal Hill.

Paragliding - Signal Hill

Experience Cape Town's stunning views with tandem paragliding at Signal Hill.

The Robben Island

Visit Robben Island, where Nelson Mandela spent 18 of his 27 years in prison.

The V&A Waterfront Cruise

The V&A Waterfront offers boat tours and cruises, starting with a sunset cruise.

The Wine Tram

The Franschhoek Wine Tram offers hop-on hop-off visits to various wine estates in the Franschhoek Valley.
